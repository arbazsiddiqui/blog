Privacy Policy

Mercuro: Thermometer Logic Puzzles · Last updated: April 2026

The short version: Mercuro requires no account and collects no personal data. Your progress lives on your device. The free version shows ads — one-time purchase removes them permanently.

Data We Store (On Your Device)

Mercuro stores your puzzle progress, solve history, streaks, and settings locally in your device's Application Support directory. This data is backed up as part of your standard iCloud Backup but is not shared across devices via iCloud Drive.

No data is ever transmitted to our servers.

No Account Required

Mercuro does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information. The app is fully functional without any login.

Daily Puzzle Generation

Daily puzzles are generated on your device using a date-based seed — the same date produces the same puzzles for all players globally. No network request is made to generate or retrieve puzzles.

Advertising

The free version of Mercuro displays ads served by Google AdMob. Before the ad SDK is initialised, Mercuro presents Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt. If you decline tracking, only contextual (non-personalised) ads are shown.

AdMob may collect device identifiers and coarse location for ad serving. Your puzzle progress and game data are never used for ad targeting.

You can remove all ads permanently with a one-time in-app purchase.

In-App Purchases

Mercuro offers in-app purchases (remove ads, hint packs) processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information.

Apple Game Center

Mercuro integrates with Apple Game Center for leaderboards and achievements. Game Center is governed by Apple's own Privacy Policy. Participation is optional and can be disabled in your iOS Settings.

Analytics

Mercuro may use an analytics service (such as Mixpanel or Amplitude) to understand aggregate usage patterns — e.g., which puzzles are most often started or abandoned. If enabled, this collects only anonymised, non-personal event data. No puzzle content, solve times linked to identifiable users, or personal information is collected.

Crash Reporting

Mercuro may use a crash reporting service to capture stack traces when the app crashes. Crash reports contain device type, iOS version, and a stack trace. They do not contain personal information or puzzle data.

Children's Privacy

Mercuro is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App Tracking Transparency framework ensures compliant ad targeting for all users.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact
