How to Play

Each puzzle is a grid of thermometers. Fill cells with mercury — always from the round bulb end upward — so that every row and column clue number is satisfied. Tap a cell to fill it, tap again to mark it empty (×), tap again to clear.

The clue badges in the margins show the number of filled cells required in each row and column. Green badge = satisfied. Red badge = overfilled.

Daily Puzzles

Three puzzles unlock every day: Warm-Up, Daily, and Challenge. They're generated on your device from the date — no internet needed. Solve all three and your streak grows.

Level Packs

The Levels tab has 800 puzzles across 8 packs (5×5 to 12×12). Chapters unlock as you complete 7 of 10 levels in the previous chapter.

Hints

Tap the lightbulb in the puzzle toolbar. A hint fills in the next logical deduction. You get 3 free hints per day; watch a short ad for an extra hint, or buy a hint pack.

My progress disappeared

Progress is stored in your device's local storage. It is included in your iCloud Backup, so if you restore your device from backup your progress should return. If you set up as a new device without restoring, progress cannot be recovered. Email us if you believe this happened unexpectedly.

Restore Purchases

Go to Settings → Restore Purchases inside the app, or visit Settings on your iPhone → App Store → Restore Purchases.
